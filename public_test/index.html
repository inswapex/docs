MetaBase Community Test Description

MetaBase is a new generation of decentralized industrial token circulation protocol platform. After 8 months of independent research and development, the platform has been initially constructed and improved. In order to encourage users to participate and verify the platform's economic system and pressure capabilities, a public beta is now open to community users.

MetaBase adopts an open operating model. Any user who has traffic and has a community can become a community node by himself through the "upgrade LP->create node" approach, without any third-party review and docking.

Basic form of public test

MetaBase's public test is dominated by community nodes. KOLs, community leaders, traffic group owners, etc. create nodes. All participating users must join the node where they are located to participate in this public test.

Public Beta Information

Open Beta Time: 2021-09-23 9:00 to 2021-09-30 16:00 (one week)

Public Beta Address: https://heco256.metabase.one/

Open Beta Nodes: Up to 5

Public Beta Reward Address: Up to 2000 addresses

Public Beta Content: Five main functions and related modules of Swap, IDO, Staking, POS, Grant

MetaBase public test basic rules

MetaBase public beta follows the following rules:

  1. With community nodes as the basic unit of each public test, the person in charge of the node organizes and feeds back related test activities. The community node should complete the organization of users, the publicity of public beta notices, and the answers to basic community questions.
  2. All users participating in the public test must join the node where they are located to participate in the public test.
  3. Public beta users can view their own public beta progress in the public beta exhibition board area of ​​the user center page, and complete all public beta tasks to receive the public beta rewards (Please note that the public beta rewards will not be issued if the tasks are not completed, please understand ).
  4. After the expiration of the public beta, platform currency rewards for users and nodes will be issued within two weeks. For specific distribution methods, please follow the announcements on Twitter and Discord in time.

Public beta user reward rules

The total number of rewards for MetaBase public beta users is 100,000 META, and the maximum reward is 2,000 user addresses, on a first-come, first-served basis, and the excess will not be issued. Public beta users must complete the following tasks at least:

  1. Swap times>3
  2. IDO times>1
  3. Staking times>1
  4. Number of POS>1
  5. Number of airdrops received>2
  6. Join the node

After completing the task, a single user address will be rewarded with 50 META (the private sale price is expected to be 0.5 USDT, which may also be adjusted)

Public test node reward rules

The MetaBase node has basic tasks such as organizing the community node to participate in the public beta, public beta announcement notification, and community-based basic question answering.

Node reward rules are as follows:

  1. If the number of community participants is more than 100, they can get a basic reward of 20000 META.
  2. Every time a user address is promoted, 20 META will be rewarded, which will be accumulated on the basis of the reward.
  3. The maximum total reward amount of the node is 40,000 META.

The public beta node needs to complete the work of "creating a node" in advance, and notify the community users of the node ID.

Node creation tutorial:

https://metabaseex.github.io/docs/tutorial/node#create-node

Community beta reward and punishment rules

  1. After technical confirmation, META rewards will not be issued to robots who swipe orders and perpetrators and nodes that do evil.
  2. During the test, the data caused by the platform failure is inaccurate, and the transaction hash can be provided to the node.
  3. All reward tokens do not need to be locked, and will be issued in batches within two weeks of the end of the public beta. The form of distribution will be notified separately.
  4. The right to interpret the public beta of MetaBase belongs to the DAO community. Any individual or node that disrupts the public beta will be permanently included in the community blacklist.
