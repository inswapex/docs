vMeta Membership Certificate

vMeta is a membership certificate of the MetaBase platform, and holders can get discounts on handling fees and related rights.

vMeta Attributes

vMeta is only an accounting symbol inside the MetaBase platform, it is not issued on the chain, and it will not be circulated or traded.

vMeta Rights

MetaBase encourages users to make contributions to the platform ecology as they can. Users can apply for casting vMeta membership certificates by destroying more than 100 META platform coins.

The more casting, the higher the user level. The initial casting ratio is 1vMeta=100 META, and the voting method of DAO governance is adopted for regular adjustment in the later period. In principle, the adjustment interval should not exceed 90 days.

vMeta is a proof of loyal users of the MetaBase platform. Holding vMeta can obtain the following rights:

  1. Get transaction fee dividends and invitation rebate dividends.

  2. INSWAP community proposal and voting rights.

  3. Step discounts on transaction fees. The more pledges, the more discounts.

  4. Market activities such as community airdrops or LP token airdrops for vMeta.

The META used by the user for casting will be entered into the black hole address on the chain and will be permanently destroyed. vMeta cannot be redeemed or transferred. Users enjoy lifetime vMeta rights and interests.
