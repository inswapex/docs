vINT Membership Certificate#

vInt is a membership certificate of the InSwap platform, and holders can get discounts on handling fees and related rights.

vInt Attributes

vInt is only an accounting symbol inside the Inswap platform, it is not issued on the chain, and it will not be circulated or traded.

vINT Rights#

InSwap encourages users to make contributions to the platform ecology as they can. Users can apply for casting vINT membership certificates by destroying more than 100 INT platform coins.

The more casting, the higher the user level. The initial casting ratio is 1vInt=100 INT, and the voting method of DAO governance is adopted for regular adjustment in the later period. In principle, the adjustment interval should not exceed 90 days.

vINT is a proof of loyal users of the Inswap platform. Holding vINT can obtain the following rights:

  1. Get transaction fee dividends and invitation rebate dividends.

  2. INSWAP community proposal and voting rights.

  3. Step discounts on transaction fees. The more pledges, the more discounts.

  4. Market activities such as community airdrops or LP token airdrops for vINT.

The INT used by the user for casting will be entered into the black hole address on the chain and will be permanently destroyed. vInt cannot be redeemed or transferred. Users enjoy lifetime vInt rights and interests.
