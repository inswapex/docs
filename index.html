InSwap Introduction#

InSwap is a layer2-based decentralized industrial token multi-chain ecological platform that supports the full ecological development of industrial tokens, including SWAP, IDO, transaction mining, staking mining, POS wealth management, community tokens, and NFT industry token circulation Wait.

Inswap adopts unique contract technology and modular development ideas to support an upgradeable DEX ecology and unlimited expansion of business modules for the first time.

InSwap supports multi-chain models such as Ethereum, HECO, and BSC. In the future, it supports mainstream public chains of other heterogeneous protocols through cross-chain bridges, and supports cross-chain circulation transactions of assets.

With a number of innovative elements, InSwap is committed to becoming a DEX platform that combines industrial tokens with the real economy, and empowers the blockchain real industry.

InSwap Features#

  • 1、 A DEX platform that combines digital tokens with industrial ecology and two-way empowerment.
  • 2、Layer2 expansion makes asset transactions more silky, fast, and low-cost.
  • 3、Based on the multi-chain structure of the cross-chain bridge, it can support cross-chain asset transactions and open up public chain data islands.
  • 4、Community autonomy, open and transparent DAO governance model, and the on-chain voting system is fair, reliable and just.
  • 5、Platform currency income regularly repurchases platform currency, which has become an important support for platform operations.
  • 6、Open the invitation and rebate mechanism to encourage users to recommend and promote contributions.
  • 7、The open IDO mechanism makes it easier to issue industrial tokens.
  • 8、The compound asset management system helps users obtain more income. The more you hold and the longer you hold the position, the higher the income.
  • 9、The multi-dimensional oracle system provides all-weather services for users inside and outside the platform.
  • 10、The black and white list mode protects the security of user assets and effectively screens qualified tokens.
