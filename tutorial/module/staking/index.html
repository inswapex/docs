MetaBase Staking Use Overview

MetaBase Staking is a decentralized, non-centralized audit staking mining system.

Any LP user can initiate a staking pool, with no audit or custody during the whole process.

Initiating a staking pool requires payment of certain platform currency fees to avoid spam and flood attacks.

Staking Notes
  1. MetaBase Staking is a non-centralized review mechanism, and the reasonable rules of the platform pose no risk to the project party and users.
  2. The creation of a staking pool is suitable for project sponsors, not for ordinary users.
  3. The staking pool is a fixed-term, fixed-income capital-guaranteed mining, and the user can unlock all the principal and income at maturity.
  4. After the staking pool is created, the project party has no right to use the funds in the pool before the pledge expires. The excess reward tokens will be automatically settled and returned after the pledge ends.

Participate in Staking

In the staking list, select the staking pool you want to participate in, click the "stake" button, fill in the pledge amount, and submit it to the chain to complete the pledge.

Staking is a fixed-term pledge. Before the pledge expires, neither the user nor the project party can intervene. After expiration, users can unlock the principal and income

::: Caution Staking Rules Description

  1. Staking participation permissions are divided into four types: public, whitelist, invitation system, and community nodes. Please confirm whether you have the permission to participate. The whitelist is suitable for large customers and specific objects (institutions, angel investors, etc.); the invitation system means that the inviter of the current account must be the creator of the staking pool; the community node means that staking is initiated at a designated community node, and only users of that node To participate.
  2. MetaBase Staking is a point-to-point, no review mechanism, and there is no risk for users and project parties.
  3. Staking is only a tool for staking mining and is not responsible for the price of the secondary market. Users must know this before participating.

:::

Create Staking Pool

Only LP users can create a staking pool. After the LP user logs in, on the home page of the Staking module, click "Create Staking Pool". Fill in the staking pool information and reward ratio to create a staking pool. After submission, the information cannot be changed. After the staking pool is created, the staking pool is in an inactive state and will not be displayed on the public list. The user first recharges staking reward tokens (according to the total amount of staking, reward ratio, and cycle calculations filled in at the time of creation), then the staking pool can be activated. After about a few minutes, the staking pool will appear in the staking list.

My Staking Pool

On the Staking homepage, click "My Stake" to view "Stake Pool I Participate in" and "Stake Pool I Created".

On the "Pledge Pool I Participate in" page, you can view the staking projects you have participated in, unlock pledge funds, and receive pledge dividends. On the "Staking Pool I Created" page, you can view the staking projects you have created and manage the staking pool.

After creating a staking pool, it is recommended to set up the LP function first, and then recharge the reward tokens.

Setting up LP must be done before activation
