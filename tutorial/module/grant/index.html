MetaBase Grant usage overview

MetaBase Grant is a decentralized, non-centralized audit token issuing system

Any LP user can initiate a Grant pool, with no audit or custody during the whole process.

Initiating the Grant pool requires payment of a certain platform currency fee to avoid junk data and flood attacks.

  1. MetaBase Grant is a non-centralized review mechanism, and the reasonable rules of the platform are no risk to the project party and users.
  2. Grant pool creation is suitable for project sponsors, not for ordinary users.
  3. The Grant pool is an unconditional token issuance mechanism, which is suitable for projects such as the issuance of tokens by project parties, the distribution of candies by large currency holders, and the undifferentiated airdrops in the community.
  4. The user receives the Grant airdrop, and the account will be received instantly with one click (except for the lock-up condition).
  5. After the Grant pool is created, the project party has no right to use the funds in the pool before the grant expires. The excess airdrop tokens will be automatically settled and returned after the airdrop expires.

Receive delivery (airdrop)

In the Grant list, select the Grant pool you want to claim, click the "Receive" button, and submit it to the chain to complete the claim.

Grant is an active delivery, and the project party cannot intervene before the grant expires. Users need to take the initiative to receive

::: Caution Grant rule description

  1. Grant participation permissions are divided into four categories: public, whitelist, invitation system, and community nodes. Please confirm whether you have the permission to participate. The whitelist is suitable for large customers and for specific objects (institutions, angel investors, etc.); the invitation system means that the inviter of the current account must be the creator of the Grant pool; the community node means that the grant is initiated at a designated community node, and only users of that node To participate.
  2. MetaBase Grant is peer-to-peer, no review mechanism, no risk for users and project parties.
  3. Grant is a community ecological tool and is not responsible for the secondary market price. Users must know this before participating.

:::

Create Grant Pool

Only LP users can create Grant pools. After the LP user logs in, on the home page of the Grant module, click "Create Airdrop".

Fill in the grant pool information to create a grant pool. After submission, the information cannot be changed. After the Grant pool is created, the Grant pool is in the inactive state and will not be displayed in the public list. The user first recharges Grant's airdrop tokens (according to the total amount of airdrop filled in at the time of creation), then the Grant pool can be activated. After about a few minutes, the Grant pool will appear in the Grant list.

My Grant

On the homepage of Grant, click "My Airdrops" to view "Airdrops I Received" and "Airdrops I Created".

On the "Airdrops I Received" page, you can view the Grant items you have received without any operation or unlocking the empty investment funds. On the "Airdrops I Created" page, you can view the created Grant projects and manage the Grant pool.

After creating the Grant pool, it is recommended to set up the LP function first, and then recharge the airdrop tokens.

Setting up LP must be done before activation
