Inswap Node Overview#

In order to encourage community autonomy, Inswap follows the autonomy principle of "rewarding income by contribution" and implements a community node system. The community node system is an open operation thinking, all users can become LP users by destroying platform coins, and then become nodes.

Community Nodes: DAO community traffic contributors, community nodes can invite projects and users to settle on the Inswap platform.

Node revenue: The node participates in the sharing of all related revenues of the platform, including but not limited to Swap fees, listing fees, IDO shares, Staking revenue management fees, POS management fees, etc.

Basic Rules Of Community Nodes
  1. Community nodes are generally community KOLs, team leaders, market operators, etc., with certain marketing capabilities and traffic, and the traffic is monetized on the Inswap platform.
  2. The name of the community node must be standardized, and illegal names (political, reactionary, yellow, insulting, etc.) must not be used, otherwise Inswap may block the node, and will not unblock and distribute profits.
  3. The community node enjoys life-long pipeline income, which is not subject to any third-party review, and is automatically settled in real time by smart contracts.
  4. The node can increase the attractiveness and influence of the node through independent operation and organization of community activities (directed IDO, staking, POS, airdrop, etc.).
  5. All nodes are anonymous nodes, building private domain traffic, users and communities. Inswap does not currently do any ranking and publicity of nodes.
  6. Any user who knows the node ID can join the node without review.

Create Node#

In the personal area of the user center page, click the navigation menu "DAO Management" -> "My Node" in the upper right corner,

On the node page, select "My Created Node". If the node has been created, the node management page will be displayed, and the "Create Node" button will not be displayed.

Click the "Create Node" button, follow the prompts on the page, fill in the node name, and submit it to the chain through Metamask to easily create a node.

Creating a node may require a certain INT fee. Do not use illegal names (reactionary, political, pornographic, insulting, etc.) for node names, otherwise they may be blocked and cause losses

Node Management#

Community nodes can easily manage their own nodes, and view node user addresses and node statistics.

Join Node#

Any user who knows the node ID can join the node.

There is only one chance to join a node, and it cannot be changed after confirmation.

After joining the node, you can participate in node activities and enjoy node benefits (airdrops, etc.).

After joining the node, check the information of the node I joined.
