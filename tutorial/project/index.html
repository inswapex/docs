MetaBase Project Ecology

The original intention of MetaBase is to face the project-friendly open DAO operation ecology, which is very rare and valuable in today's blockchain industry.

MetaBase provides a free, fair, just, audit-free, and non-custodial operation platform for the majority of project parties.

Basic Principle

  1. Project listing does not require review and independent listing. Any project needs to pay a certain amount of platform currency as a platform fee to screen high-quality assets and reduce the interference of invalid long-tail currencies.

  2. The listing means that you have all the ecological tools of MetaBase, and you can use any module freely.

  3. The project party initiates IDO crowdfunding, Staking pledge mining, POS current mining, community launch, etc. on MetaBase without review. After paying a certain platform currency fee, it will be automatically launched. There is no third-party intervention and conditional approval during the whole process.

Project Listing

The listing of the project is submitted by any user in the project community, and the on-chain authentication is initiated to the contract, which is automatically completed.

In the left navigation bar of the MetaBase App homepage, click "Token Authentication",

In the pop-up information box, enter the token address (required) and the recommended node ID (beginning with 0x, optional),

Click "Submit". Then submit the link in Metamask.

  1. Project listing does not require third-party approval or approval. The listing fee is automatically deducted from the balance of the currently logged-in account.
  2. All functional modules of the platform can be used after the currency is listed.
  3. Malicious tokens, fraudulent tokens, or token names that violate the public order of politics, history, etc., once discovered, may be included in the token blacklist. Currency listing accounts may be blocked to maintain the order of the community.
  4. Token contracts may have certain security risks, please select and identify yourself. MetaBase cannot determine and cannot confirm related responsibilities.
